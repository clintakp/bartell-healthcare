Our Services

We specialize in comprehensive chiropractic care with a multidisciplinary, integrated approach. Our focus is on diagnosing and treating spine-related disorders and painful joint issues. By combining traditional manual techniques with advanced therapies, we aim to decrease pain, improve movement, and enhance overall well-being.

We accept most health plans, personal injury, and workers' compensation cases.
Manual Adjustive Techniques

Traditional hands-on chiropractic adjustments to restore proper alignment and relieve pain.

Cox Flexion-Distraction

Gentle, non-invasive spinal decompression to alleviate pressure on discs and nerves.

Cold Laser (Erchonia)

Low-level laser therapy to reduce inflammation, promote healing, and relieve pain.

Custom Functional Orthotics

Custom foot levelers designed to correct imbalances and improve posture.

Exercise & Fitness Protocols

Personalized fitness programs to enhance mobility, strength, and overall health.

Myofascial Release

Fast, accurate diagnostic imaging with results available within minutes.
